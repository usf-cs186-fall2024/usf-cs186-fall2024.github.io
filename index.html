CS 186 Fall 2024

Welcome to CS 186 Building CS Skills & Community for the Fall 2024 semester. This is a fun and engaging course for first-year CS majors to learn about and participate in the CS communities on and off campus, launch their professional profiles, as well as develop core technical skills.

This course is recommended for introverts and extroverts, with or without programming experience! There are several reasons why you should register:

  • It will be fun! You will earn course credit for participating in CS events on-campus and in the SF Bay Area, including CS socials with free pizza, special guest speakers, or even attending an industry conference!

  • It will prepare you for the CS major! You will learn several key "missing semester" skills that will supplement your first CS major courses.

  • It will set you up for success! You gain significant educational benefits from evidenced-based high-impact practices like first-year experiences.

Email the instructor at sjengle@cs.usfca.edu if you have any questions or need help registering.
